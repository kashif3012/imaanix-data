Privacy Policy for Imaanix

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Imaanix ("we", "our", or "us") provides Islamic quote and status content. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account signup and we do not intentionally collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Imaanix stores limited app data locally on your device, including:

  • Favorite quote IDs
  • Cached quote data for offline use and update performance
  • Quote content version and last sync date

This local data is used only to provide app features and is not sold.

3. Internet Access and Remote Content

The app may fetch quote/version updates from a remote source over the internet. This helps keep content current. Standard technical request data (such as IP address and user-agent) may be processed by your network provider or hosting infrastructure as part of normal web requests.

4. Sharing and Export

When you use save/share features, the app generates image output on your device. If you choose to share, content is passed to the platform share sheet or selected app by your action.

5. Third-Party Services

Imaanix may rely on platform or hosting services required for app operation and content delivery. We do not sell personal data.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable steps to reduce risk, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Imaanix is not directed to children under 13 to collect personal data. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us so we can review and remove it where applicable.

8. Your Choices

  • You can clear app data from device settings to remove cached/favorite data.
  • You can stop content updates by using the app offline.
  • You control if and where any generated image is shared.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: imaanix.app@gmail.com
